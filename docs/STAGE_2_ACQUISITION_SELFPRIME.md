## Strategy

Selfprime is the "first domino" in the Factory portfolio. Practitioner segment is the highest-intent, fastest-to-conversion entry point within Selfprime's ICP.

### Target Profile: Selfprime Practitioner

**Definition:** Professional astrologers, life coaches, wellness practitioners, and embodiment facilitators (30–70yo, $50k–$250k annual gross) who actively maintain a website, social media, or client CRM and charge for services.

**Key signals:**
- Owns or co-owns a practice (solopreneur or 2–5 person team)
- Offers personalized readings/coaching (not just content)
- Uses some form of CRM, email list, or scheduling software (Calendly, Acuity, Dubsado)
- Active on Instagram, Facebook, TikTok, or Threads (niche community presence)
- Already paying for Substack, Circle, Mighty Networks, or similar $15–50/mo tools

**Why this segment:**
- Highest Lifetime Value (LTV) potential: recurring $50–200/mo willingness if ROI is clear
- Lowest CAC: already aggregated in practitioner communities, Reddit, Discord, astrology sites
- Fastest payoff: real bookings drive retention (unlike hobbyists or students)
- Cross-sell ready: packaging → Selfprime Studio once they hit 3+ services

**Messaging fix:**
- Change: "AI-powered personal brand" → "Understand yourself. Share your truth."
- Change: "Automated human design reading" → "Birth chart exploration tool"
