## 1. Selection criteria

A candidate qualifies only if **all** of the following can be verified publicly:

| # | Criterion | Public signal |
|---|---|---|
| 1 | **Active paying practice** | Public booking page / rate card / waitlist; sessions priced $80–$300 |
| 2 | **Audience signal** | ≥1k followers on one channel, OR ≥1 podcast appearance in the last 24 months, OR active newsletter (≥monthly), OR conference speaker |
| 3 | **Lineage signal** | Trained at or cites a named school (Demetra George, Forrest Centre, Astrology Hub, NCGR, ISAR, Jovian Archive, IHDS, Genetic Matrix), OR holds a recognized credential (NCGR-PAA, ISAR CAP) |
| 4 | **Voice fit** | Public material demonstrates analytical or operational framing — e.g. talks about chart synthesis method, client workflow, business of practice. NOT purely devotional / decorative / "vibes" content |
| 5 | **Reachability** | Public business email or contact form on website. **Not** DM-only |
| 6 | **Open to tools** | Has posted publicly about software, AI, scheduling tools, or the operational side of their practice — or at minimum has not posted explicit hostility to AI |

**Disqualifiers** (any one excludes):
- Public material suggests they would object to AI co-pilots on principle (anti-AI stance, "human-only" pledge)
- No respectful public path to contact (DM-only, paywalled-only)
- Deceased, on extended hiatus, or retired (where known)
- Already shipping a competing chart-AI product (would be a competitor, not a partner)
