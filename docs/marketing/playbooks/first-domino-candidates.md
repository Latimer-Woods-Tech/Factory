## 1. Selection criteria

A candidate qualifies only if **all** of the following can be verified publicly:

| # | Criterion | Public signal |
|---|---|---|
| 1 | **Active paying practice** | Public booking page / rate card / waitlist; sessions priced $80–$300 |
| 2 | **Audience signal** | ≥1k followers on one channel, OR ≥1 podcast appearance in the last 24 months, OR active newsletter (≥monthly), OR conference speaker |
| 3 | **Lineage signal** | Trained at or cites a named school (Demetra George, Forrest Centre, Astrology Hub, NCGR, ISAR, Jovian Archive, IHDS, Genetic Matrix), OR holds a recognized credential (NCGR-PAA, ISAR CAP) |
| 4 | **Voice fit** | Public material demonstrates analytical or operational framing — e.g. talks about chart synthesis method, client workflow, business of practice. NOT purely devotional / decorative / "vibes" content |
| 5 | **Reachability** | Public business email or contact form on website. **Not** DM-only |
| 6 | **Open to tools** | Has posted publicly about software, AI, scheduling tools, or the operational side of their practice — or at minimum has not posted explicit hostility to AI |

**Disqualifiers** (any one excludes):
- Public material suggests they would object to AI co-pilots on principle (anti-AI stance, "human-only" pledge)
- No respectful public path to contact (DM-only, paywalled-only)
- Deceased, on extended hiatus, or retired (where known)
- Already shipping a competing chart-AI product (would be a competitor, not a partner)

## 5. Operator workflow

Three steps. This list does **not** enter the autonomous outreach queue ([`packages/crm/`](../../../packages/crm/) warm path) until step 3.

1. **Review and replace.** Read every row in §3. Remove anyone who looks wrong from current public information (deceased, retired, pivoted, hostile to AI). Replace every `TBD —` placeholder with a real name pulled from the source index linked in that row. Aim for 30 confirmed candidates total.

2. **Verify public contact info for Tier 1.** For each of the 10 Tier 1 candidates, find a public business email or contact form. **No email = no outreach** — per [`../CONSTITUTION.md §5`](../CONSTITUTION.md#5-channel-allowlist--readiness-gates) "Cold mass outbound (>10 recipients per day across all sequences) without prior consent on file" is denylisted; respectful warm-style outreach to publicly published contact addresses is permitted only when the address is genuinely public. Document each contact path on the row. If you cannot find one in 5 minutes of searching, that row becomes Tier 3 (test bucket) — don't force it.

3. **Approve.** Commit this file with status changed from `🟡 draft` to `🟢 approved — Tier 1 ready for outreach queue`. The marketing supervisor ([PR 3e](../pr3-briefs/3e-marketing-supervisor.md), once shipped) reads this file and pulls the Tier 1 rows into its outreach queue using the `prime_self:practitioner` voice profile. Tiers 2 and 3 stay in this file as later-quarter targets — they do **not** auto-enter the queue.

**Honesty discipline (per [`CONSTITUTION.md §9`](../CONSTITUTION.md#9-honesty--truth)):** If at step 1 you can't fill 30 rows in good conscience, ship the file with 12 or 18 — under-counting is honesty; over-counting is debt. The bootstrap goal in ICP §2 is **5 design-partner Zoom calls**, not 30. Tier 1 needs 10 viable names — that's the only hard floor.
